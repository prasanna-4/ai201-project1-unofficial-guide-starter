Rate My Professors — Brookfield University
Professor: Anita Reyes
Department: Computer Science
Course commonly reviewed: CS 420 Machine Learning
Overall rating: 4.3 / 5  | Would take again: 74%  | Difficulty: 4.6 / 5

---
Review 1  (CS 420, Fall)  Quality 5 / 5  Difficulty 5 / 5
Do NOT take Reyes until you have finished linear algebra. The first two weeks are matrix
calculus and if you are shaky on vectors and matrices you will be lost immediately. Once you
have the math, she is an outstanding teacher. Fast-paced and no curve, so stay on top of it.

---
Review 2  (CS 420, Spring)  Quality 4 / 5  Difficulty 5 / 5
The prereqs matter. Officially you need CS 310, but the real prereq is being comfortable with
linear algebra and probability. There is no curve — your grade is exactly what you earn. The
projects (implementing gradient descent and a small neural net) are the best part.

---
Review 3  (CS 420, Fall)  Quality 4 / 5  Difficulty 4 / 5
Fast pace, heavy math, but incredibly rewarding. Take linear algebra first, seriously.
Reyes is responsive and her lecture notes are detailed. No curve means partial credit on the
math derivations is your friend — show all your work.

---
Review 4  (CS 420, Spring)  Quality 4 / 5  Difficulty 5 / 5
Loved this class but it nearly broke me because I took it before finishing my math
requirements. If I could redo it I would take linear algebra and the stats course first.
The content is current and she brings in real research examples.
