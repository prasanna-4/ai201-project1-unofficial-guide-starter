Rate My Professors — Brookfield University
Professor: David Okafor
Department: Computer Science
Course commonly reviewed: CS 101 Introduction to Programming
Overall rating: 4.8 / 5  | Would take again: 96%  | Difficulty: 2.1 / 5

---
Review 1  (CS 101, Fall)  Quality 5 / 5  Difficulty 2 / 5
If you have NEVER written a line of code in your life, take Okafor. He explains everything
from absolute zero and never makes you feel stupid for asking. Perfect for complete beginners.
The pace is gentle and he holds extra office hours before every exam.

---
Review 2  (CS 101, Spring)  Quality 5 / 5  Difficulty 2 / 5
The most patient professor I have ever had. Grading is lenient — partial credit on everything,
and he drops your lowest quiz. Great first CS class. My only warning: because it is so gentle,
the jump to CS 201 with Marsh is a shock. Enjoy the easy semester but keep practicing.

---
Review 3  (CS 101, Fall)  Quality 5 / 5  Difficulty 1 / 5
Genuinely kind human. Lectures are clear, slides are detailed, and he records every class.
Beginners thrive here. If you already know how to program you might be bored, but for
first-timers there is no better intro instructor at Brookfield.

---
Review 4  (CS 101, Summer)  Quality 4 / 5  Difficulty 2 / 5
Easy A if you show up and do the labs. Lots of hand-holding which is exactly what a beginner
needs. Office hours are rarely crowded and he answers email within hours.
