Rate My Professors — Brookfield University
Professor: Susan Lin
Department: Computer Science
Course commonly reviewed: CS 340 Operating Systems
Overall rating: 4.0 / 5  | Would take again: 71%  | Difficulty: 4.8 / 5

---
Review 1  (CS 340, Fall)  Quality 4 / 5  Difficulty 5 / 5
This is the most project-heavy class in the major. You build pieces of a real OS in C on top
of the xv6 codebase. Expect 15+ hours a week on the projects alone. Brutal workload, but I
came out a dramatically better programmer. Worth it if you can spare the time.

---
Review 2  (CS 340, Spring)  Quality 4 / 5  Difficulty 5 / 5
The projects are done in groups of three and pick your group carefully — a weak partner will
sink you. Lin's exams are fair and based on the projects and lectures. The C and pointer-heavy
debugging is where most people lose hours. Do not take this with two other hard classes.

---
Review 3  (CS 340, Fall)  Quality 5 / 5  Difficulty 5 / 5
Hardest and most rewarding class at Brookfield. You will hate the kernel debugging at 2am and
then love it when your scheduler finally works. Lin is responsive on the forum and the project
specs are detailed. Make sure your C is solid before you start.

---
Review 4  (CS 340, Spring)  Quality 3 / 5  Difficulty 5 / 5
Incredible learning but the time sink is real. The group projects dominate your semester.
If your C is rusty, review pointers and memory management before week one or you will drown.
