Rate My Professors — Brookfield University
Professor: Tom Becker
Department: Computer Science
Course commonly reviewed: CS 330 Database Systems
Overall rating: 3.4 / 5  | Would take again: 62%  | Difficulty: 1.8 / 5

---
Review 1  (CS 330, Fall)  Quality 3 / 5  Difficulty 2 / 5
Easiest A in the CS department. Becker reads the slides almost verbatim and the exams are
open-note. The downside: you will not learn much beyond basic SQL. If you want an easy
semester or need to balance a hard courseload, this is the class.

---
Review 2  (CS 330, Spring)  Quality 4 / 5  Difficulty 1 / 5
Low effort, high grade. Homework is light and the final is a take-home. Honestly I wish it
were more rigorous — we barely touched indexing or query optimization. Good GPA booster, weak
on depth.

---
Review 3  (CS 330, Fall)  Quality 3 / 5  Difficulty 2 / 5
Lectures are just slide-reading, so attendance is optional and most people skip. The SQL
assignments are straightforward. Take it if you want a guaranteed good grade, not if you
actually want to master databases.

---
Review 4  (CS 330, Summer)  Quality 4 / 5  Difficulty 2 / 5
Chill professor, chill class. Open-note exams, generous deadlines. You learn enough SQL to
put it on a resume but not much about how databases work under the hood.
